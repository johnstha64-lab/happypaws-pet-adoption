Adoption Information

Adopting a pet is an important decision. At Happy Paws Pet Adoption, we aim to make the process simple, safe, and suitable for both the animal and the adopter.

Adoption Process

  1. View the available pets on our website.
  2. Submit an enquiry through the Contact Us page.
  3. Meet the pet and speak with our adoption team.
  4. Complete a simple suitability check.
  5. Take your new pet home after approval.

Before Adopting

  • Make sure your home is safe for a pet.
  • Prepare food, bedding, toys, and basic supplies.
  • Consider the cost of vet care and daily feeding.
  • Ensure you have enough time to care for the animal.

Adoption Conditions

Applicants should be responsible, caring, and ready for a long-term commitment. Some pets may need special care depending on their age, health, or behaviour.
