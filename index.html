Welcome to Our Website

Happy Paws Pet Adoption is a local animal rescue organisation dedicated to helping homeless cats and dogs find safe, loving, and permanent homes.

Every year, thousands of pets are abandoned or surrendered to shelters. At Happy Paws, we provide medical care, food, shelter, and rehabilitation until these animals are ready to be adopted.

Why Adopt a Pet?

  • Provide a loving home.
  • Reduce homelessness among pets.
  • Support animal welfare.
  • Gain a loyal companion.

Our Mission

Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate and rehome animals while promoting responsible pet ownership.
