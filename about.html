About Us

Happy Paws Pet Adoption is a non-profit animal rescue organisation dedicated to helping homeless, abandoned, and neglected pets find loving homes. Our organisation works closely with volunteers, foster carers, veterinarians, and local communities to improve the lives of animals in need.

We rescue cats and dogs from shelters, provide medical treatment and rehabilitation, and carefully match them with suitable families. Our goal is to reduce the number of homeless pets while encouraging responsible pet ownership.

Pet adoption is important because it gives animals a second chance at life. By adopting a rescue pet, families help reduce overcrowding in shelters and support animal welfare in the community.

Our Values

  • Compassion for animals
  • Responsible pet ownership
  • Community involvement
  • Animal welfare and safety
